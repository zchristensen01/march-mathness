PROMPT TO SEND TO CLAUDE RESEARCH
==================================

For the 2026 NCAA Men's Basketball Tournament, I need stats for all 68 tournament teams.

Give me a CSV table with these exact columns:

Team, Seed, Region, NET_Rank, Quad1_Wins, Last_10_Wins, Conf_Tourney_Champion

Column definitions:

- Team: The team's full name. See the TEAM NAME LIST at the bottom of this prompt. Use ONLY names from that list. If a tournament team's name doesn't exactly match anything on the list, use the closest match and put an asterisk next to it so I can fix it.

- Seed: Tournament seed, integer 1 through 16.

- Region: The region name exactly as announced in the bracket (for example East, West, South, Midwest -- use whatever the actual region names are this year).

- NET_Rank: The team's current NCAA NET ranking (integer 1-365). The official source is ncaa.com/rankings/basketball-men/d1/ncaa-mens-basketball-net-rankings. Every D1 team has a NET rank.

- Quad1_Wins: Number of wins vs Quad 1 opponents. Quad 1 means games against NET ranked 1-30 at home, 1-50 on neutral court, or 1-75 on the road. You can find this on warrennolan.com or ESPN team pages under the schedule/results tab.

- Last_10_Wins: Just the number of wins in the team's last 10 games, counting conference tournament games. For example if they went 8-2 in their last 10, just put 8.

- Conf_Tourney_Champion: Put 1 if this team won their conference tournament. Put 0 if they did not. There are 32 conference tournaments total.

Sources to check: ESPN team pages, warrennolan.com, NCAA.com/rankings, the bracket announcement itself.

Output ONLY the CSV with a header row and 68 data rows. No explanations or extra text.

Also separately list the 4 First Four play-in matchups with the seed and region for each.

I need the complete 2026 NCAA Men's Basketball Tournament bracket as a JSON file.

Use this exact format:

  "year": 2026,

    {"team": "Team Name", "seed": 1, "region": "Region1Name", "slot": 1},

    ...
  ]

Rules for the teams array:

Each region has exactly 16 slots numbered 1 through 16. The slot number determines who plays who in the first round:

  Slots 1 and 2: 1-seed vs 16-seed
  Slots 3 and 4: 8-seed vs 9-seed
  Slots 5 and 6: 5-seed vs 12-seed
  Slots 7 and 8: 4-seed vs 13-seed
  Slots 9 and 10: 6-seed vs 11-seed
  Slots 11 and 12: 3-seed vs 14-seed
  Slots 13 and 14: 7-seed vs 10-seed
  Slots 15 and 16: 2-seed vs 15-seed

The higher seed (lower number) always gets the lower slot number. So the 1-seed is slot 1 and the 16-seed is slot 2.

For First Four play-in games, list the team that appears in the bracket with these extra fields:

  {"team": "Team A", "seed": 16, "region": "East", "slot": 2, "play_in": true, "play_in_opponent": "Team B"}

Both play-in teams should be included this way (one entry per play-in matchup -- use the team listed first in the bracket as the main entry and the other as play_in_opponent).

There should be 4 regions, 16 slots per region, for a total of 64 entries in the teams array (plus the play-in opponents noted in the play_in_opponent fields).

Use the exact region names from the bracket announcement.

IMPORTANT: Use ONLY team names from the list below. If a team name does not exactly match, use the closest match and mark it with an asterisk in a comment.

Output ONLY the JSON. No explanations.

HOW THE SYSTEM USES THIS FILE
==============================

The slot numbering defines the entire bracket tree:

Round of 64: Slot pairs play each other (1v2, 3v4, 5v6, etc.)
Round of 32: Winner of slots 1-2 plays winner of slots 3-4, winner of 5-6 plays winner of 7-8, etc.
Sweet 16: Winners keep advancing through the bracket tree
Elite 8: Two remaining teams per region
Final Four: Region winners play each other
Championship: Final Four winners

Play-in games are resolved first by the simulation engine before the Round of 64 begins. After the real play-in games happen on Tuesday/Wednesday, update this file to remove the play_in and play_in_opponent fields from the winning teams.
